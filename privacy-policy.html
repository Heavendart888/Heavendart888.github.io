Privacy Policy for IDS Vault

HeavenDart respects your privacy. IDS Vault is built on a strict "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. Everything you save in IDS Vault stays exclusively on your local device.

1. Information Collection & Permissions

To function securely, IDS Vault requires the following local device permissions. None of this data is transmitted to us:

  • Camera: Used strictly to capture images of your ID cards directly into the app. These photos are encrypted instantly and never appear in your phone's public gallery.
  • Storage / File Access: Used only to read and write your encrypted documents to your device's local storage.
  • Biometric Authentication: The app utilizes your device's native hardware (Fingerprint/Face ID) to unlock the vault. We do not collect, access, or store your actual biometric data.

2. Data Security & Encryption

All documents are encrypted locally using military-grade AES-256 encryption. The unique encryption key is stored securely in your device's hardware keystore. Because we have no cloud servers and no access to your device, we cannot view, recover, or reset your encrypted files under any circumstances.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

IDS Vault operates completely offline. We do not use third-party analytics, cloud backups, or advertising trackers. Your data is never shared, sold, or transmitted to us or any third parties.
